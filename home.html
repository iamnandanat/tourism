Packages We Offer

At EpicEscapeTravels,we offer a variety of tailored travel packages to suit every type of traveler.Whether you're seeking a relaxing beach vacations,an adventurous hiking trip,or a cultural city exploration,we have something for you.Our packages include:Beach and Relaxation,Family-Friendly Adventure,Luxury Safari Experience,Food and Wine Tour,Pilgrimage or Spiritual Journey,Ski & Snow Adventure.

Beach and Relaxation

Destination:Maldives or Bali.

Highlights:Private overwater bungalows,crystal-clear waters,white santy beaches.

Duration:7-10days.

Activities:Diving,spa treatments,sunset yoga and gourmet dining.

Family-Friendly Adventure

Destination:Orlando,Florida(Disney world & Universal studios)

Highlights:Family-friendly accommodations,entertainment.

Duration:7-10 days.

Activities:Visits to theme parks,character meet-and-greets,water parks,and interactive exhibits.

Luxury Safari Experience

Destination:Kenya or South Africa

Highlights:Luxury lodges and private safaris in the Maasai Mara or Kruger National Park.

Duration:7-10 days.

Activities:Game drives,hot air baloon safaris,wildlife photography,and local cultural experiences.

Food and Wine Tour

Destination:Italy(Tuscany, Rome, Florence)

Highlights:Wine tasting in vineyards,food markets,cooking classes.

Duration:7-10 days.

Activities:Wine tasting,olive oil tours,cooking classes,and food excursions in market and restaurants.

Pilgrimage or Spiritual Journey

Destination:India(Varanasi, Rishikesh, Bodh Gaya)

Highlights:Temples,spiritual retreats,yoga,and meditation.

Duration:10-14 days.

Activities:Temple visits,meditation retreats,Ganges boat rides,and yoga class.

Ski and Snow Adventure

Destination:Switzerland or Aspen,USA

Highlights:Ski resorts,luxury chalets,and snow sports.

Duration:7-10 days.

Activities:Skiing, snowboarding, snowshoeing, and apres-ski activities.
